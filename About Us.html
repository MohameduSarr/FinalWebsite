My name is Mohamedu Sarr, and I began working on the "Onslaught" series in 2020. During the Covid-19 pandemic, I decided to learn how to draw and make my own comic. I started working on my artskills and by 2022, I was confident in them. I modeled my comic based off of the Japanese style known as "Manga". I studied this style and their masters, and soon, I was quite good at it. This series is my passion project. It is my dream for this series to one day be known amoungst manga fans and to be popular around the world. I know it can, an with your help and support, it is possible!

I'm grateful for the continued support of my fans and I hope to deliver even more amazing content in the future! Thank you!
