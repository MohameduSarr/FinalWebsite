The Onlsaught Manga is a webcomic published by Mohamedu Sarr on the website Global Comix. It has been in seralization since September 2022 and is currently being updated every month. The story follows a man (Kendo Komidachi) who is on a quest to execute his brother's last wish before he died. He is willing to complete this mission by any means nessesary.
